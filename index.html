ABOUT US

The Bhongulethu Foundation is a community development non-profit organisation provoding distinguished programmes and transformative spaces for the progressive realisation of equality. In this mission we have made it our primary objectives to deliver progressive programmes in early childhood development, quality primary schooling and adult education to those who need it most. We support and expand on these objectives with supporting projects in access to nutrition, recreation, child safety, gender equality and healthcare, sustain our value cycle and driving the impact to create change!

OUR IMPACT

Children's Eyes

Eyes of Africa spent 2 days testing the children's eyes at Bhongolethu. This was a fascinating and exciting opportunity for the children. Before Eyes of Africa came, most of the children had never had their eyes checked or tested! This is a great way to help the children further so they can get the support they need for a clearer vision. #eyesofafrica #charity #education #nonprofit #actionchange Eyes of Africa Action Change.

Mandela Day

The day started with tree planting. We were gifted with 23 fruit trees. They have been planted in such a way that they will create a little forest for the children to enjoy once established. After tree planting the teams worked on fixing some of our playground equipment while the kids played soccer and jumped on the jumping castles with the volunteers from Madrid. The smell of traditional boerewors cooking on the braai engulfed our senses and then filled the hungry tummies. @actioningchange @evolveabroad @leaglobalpathways and In2Africa really know how to spoil the children at Bhongolethu, especially for Mandela day! Thank you spending much more than 67 minutes on us today.
